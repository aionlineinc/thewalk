# Page copy & layout structure: theWalk Redesign

*Use this doc to align **design, layout, and engineering** with **page-level copy**. Pair with `agents/copywriter.md`, `docs/design-intake.md`, `docs/feature-scope.md`, and `docs/asset-inventory.md`.*

**Client:** theWalk Ministries Inc · **Design mode:** clone

---

## Intake-derived hints (IA starting points)

**Product summary** (overall story for marketing/home):
The project involves redesigning theWalk.org to create a clean, modern, and content-driven website that aligns with the updated ministry structure and vision of theWalk Ministries Inc. This Christian organization is focused on discipleship, fellowship, and spiritual development, aiming to foster a kingdom-focused culture. The redesign will emphasize simplicity and clarity, reflecting the minimal and clean style family, and will be inspired by examples like Superpower and Cure Real Estate. The goal is to enhance user experience and communication, ensuring the website effectively supports the ministry's mission and vision.

---

**Core workflows** — treat each major step as a candidate **screen, page, or modal** (design copy + layout per step):
•	Discover ministry → understand identity → choose pathway
	•	Cross Over → entry / transformation
	•	Cross Roads → growth / decision-making
	•	Cross Connect → community / impact

Additional:
	•	Browse teachings → engage → return
	•	Learn → reflect → take action

---

**Must-have features** — group into **pages or hubs**; each group needs a primary message and CTA:
•	Structured pathway sections (Cross Over, Cross Roads, Cross Connect)
	•	Clear journey-based navigation
	•	Media/teaching delivery
	•	Responsive design
	•	CMS-driven content
	•	Clear CTA flows

---

**Copy priorities (from intake)** — use when naming routes and sections in `page-copy-and-structure.md`:
•	Transformation is a journey
	•	Structure leads to growth
	•	Identity → Decision → Community
	•	Cross Over (entry point)
	•	Cross Roads (decision & development)
	•	Cross Connect (community & impact)

	•	“Your journey starts here”
	•	“Walk in purpose”
	•	“Grow through structure”

---

**Audience** (tone and reading level per surface):
Members of theWalk Ministries, potential new members interested in Christian discipleship and spiritual development, and visitors seeking information about the ministry's programs and events. The site will also pay special interest in established ministries seeking to become apart of the fellowship as well as businesses and potential donors seeking to support its initiatives.

---

**Surfaces:** web, API — repeat or adapt this blueprint per platform if layout differs.

Matthew 23:11, 1 Corinthians 3:11

**Headline:** DISCIPLESHIP | FELLOWSHIP

Luke 10:1

**Headline:** FOCUS
**Body:** Decisions made from those at lower levels of the structure will be focused at providing benefit to those who reside at the top tiers of the structure, so that beneficial priority for decisions made will proceed down from the top. By this we emphasis Jesus’ evangelistic focus where the physician comes to the sick and not those who are well.
Mark 2:17
